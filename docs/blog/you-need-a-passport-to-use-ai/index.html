Remember the whole theater around “let’s stop Mythos, it’s too dangerous”? What did I tell you? President Trump himself said it: they were going to start asking for your documents. And now the same thing is happening with OpenAI.

This is shaping up as a kind of token bank - a service you have to register with, ID in hand, before you’re allowed to spend your tokens. You need a passport to use AI.
